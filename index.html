About the Sandbox

A Structured Space for Financial Innovation

The NRB Regulatory Sandbox is a controlled and supervised testing environment, enabling innovators to test their products, services, or solutions prior to full-scale launch. It promotes safe and responsible innovations while deepening financial inclusion through new technology-enabled products.


The Sandbox serves as a mechanism for regulatory engagement with emerging FinTech innovators, helping both innovators and the central bank better understand risks, strengthen consumer protection, and build market confidence.

Key Pillars

Our Objectives & Audience

Objectives

To foster financial inclusion, promote responsible innovation, reduce barriers to entry for FinTech solutions, and strengthen Nepal's financial ecosystem through structured regulatory flexibility and close collaboration between innovators and the Central Bank.

Target Audience

  • Licensed Banks & Financial Institutions
  • Licensed Payment-related Institutions
  • Licensed Remittance Companies
  • New FinTech Companies incorporated in Nepal
