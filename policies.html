Nepal Rastra Bank Act, 2058

The Nepal Rastra Bank Act, 2058 (2002) is the foundational legislation establishing Nepal Rastra Bank as the central bank of Nepal. This Act defines NRB's powers, functions, duties, and its mandate to regulate and supervise the financial system in Nepal.

Payment and Settlement Act, 2075

The Payment and Settlement Act, 2075 (2019) governs payment and settlement systems in Nepal, including the licensing and regulation of payment system operators, payment service providers, and related entities. This Act is particularly relevant for FinTech innovators working on payment solutions.

Foreign Exchange Regulations Act, 2019

The Foreign Exchange Regulations Act governs all foreign exchange transactions in Nepal. This is particularly relevant for Sandbox participants dealing with cross-border remittance, international payment solutions, or any product involving foreign currency flows.
